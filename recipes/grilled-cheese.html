Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

Two slices of bread, some butter and cheese make a masterpiece of simple lol

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread - your choice
  • Butter or Mayo (the real stuff)
  • 2 slices of cheese - you pick, there's so many

Instructions

  1. Heat a pan
  2. Spread butter or mayo on one side of each slice of bread
  3. Add cheese to unbuttered side of bread
  4. Combine slices of bread to form a sandwich
  5. Cook sandwich in hot pan; flipping so that each side is golden brown (or burnt, no judgement here)
